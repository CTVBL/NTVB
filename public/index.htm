WELCOME TO NEW TORONTO VOLLEYBALL  

NEW TORONTO VOLLEYBALL is an indoor co-ed volleyball league for adults. The league is based in Toronto Ontario. Games are held Tuesday evenings from 6:45pm to 9:30pm at local public schools around Yonge & Eglinton and Don Mills & Sheppard. The new season will be starting October 7, 2014 and it will run until April 14, 2015.

2014/15 SEASON BEGINS

The season will begin Tuesday, October 7.
