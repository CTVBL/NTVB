WELCOME TO CENTRAL TORONTO VOLLEYBALL  

CENTRAL TORONTO VOLLEYBALL is an indoor co-ed volleyball league for adults. The league is based in Toronto Ontario. Games are held Tuesday evenings from 6:45pm to 9:30pm at local public schools around Bayview & Eglinton, Bayview & Sheppard, and Lawrence & Avenue. The new season will be starting October 4, 2016 and it will run until April 25, 2017.

2016/17 SEASON STARTS SOON

The regular season will begin Tuesday, October 4, 2016 and there is an open night of play on Tuesday, September 27, 2016. Please check the schedule for where your team is playing.



 

LOCAL PUBLIC EATERY
Come to Local Public Eatery after volleyball on Tuesdays for food and drinks. The Leaside location is at 180 Laird Dr., just south of Eglinton Ave.
