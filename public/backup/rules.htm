TORONTO NORTH VOLLEYBALL LEAGUE Rules and Regulations

Ontario Volleyball Association rules apply except as follows:

  • Players must be 19 years of age or older.

  • There will be no volleyball during March Break or Christmas holidays.

  • There will be four teams playing at each location, on a rotating basis.

  • All teams should arrive at the gym at 6:45 regardless of when they are scheduled to play.

  • All teams, in all divisions must supply referees, lines persons and score keepers. This will speed things up and should ensure you are able to play all your games.

  • Time permitting, each team will play 5 games to 25 points using rally point scoring. A team must win by 2 points or more. A game will go to a maximum of 27 points.

  • Teams not playing should refrain from playing volleyball, basketball, using the school equipment, etc while games are being played in the gym.

  • No food or garbage should be left in the gym. This helps us keep our permits.

  • Court shoes (running shoes) must be worn in the gym.

  • No kicking of the volleyball is allowed.

  • "Let" serves are permitted (i.e. ball is allowed to hit the net on a serve if it goes over)

  • Penetration under the net is allowed if no incidental contact is made.

  • 5 service maximum for Recreational games.

  • If any team DEFAULTS three evening of play, that team will be disqualified from the league and the teams entry fee will be forfeited.

  • Teams will play on a 2, 2, 1 rotating basis. (i.e. Play 2, Sit 2, Play 2, Sit 2, Play 1, Sit 1). If agreed upon by all captains, this rotation can be altered as needed.

  • Competitive 4's: 3 male, 1 female (minimum of 1 female on the court)

  • Intermediate 4's: 2 male, 2 female (minimum of 2 females on the court)

  • Recreational 6's: 3 male, 2 female (minimum of 2 females on the court)

ALL CONCERNS AND DISPUTES ARE SUBJECT T0 THE DISCRETION OF THE LEAGUE EXECUTIVE.
