Recreation Playoffs

 

All Recreation playoff games will be held at Eglinton Public School.  Teams not playing should supply referees, lines and score people.  The current standings are:

1. Erasers

2. Amigos

3. Superstars

 

Semi-Finals – Best 3 of 5

Game 1: Amigos vs. Superstars

Game 2: Superstars vs. Amigos

Game 3: Amigos vs. Superstars

Game 4: Superstars vs. Amigos (if necessary)

Game 5: Amigos vs. Superstars (if necessary)

 

Finals – Best 3 of 5

Game 1: Erasers vs. Winner of Amigos / Superstars

Game 2: Winner of Amigos / Superstars vs. Erasers

Game 3: Erasers vs. Winner of Amigos / Superstars

Game 4: Winner of Amigos / Superstars vs. Erasers (if necessary)

Game 5: Erasers vs. Winner of Amigos / Superstars (if necessary)

 

·        Each game will be rally-point scoring to 25 points, win by 2, cap at 27.

·        Home team serves first (e.g. Game 1, Amigos serve first).

·        If you have time at the end of the finals, just play for fun.
