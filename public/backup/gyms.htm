Gymnasiums

Listed below are the public school gymnasiums available for volleyball this season.  This list will be updated once more prior to the season to reflect all schools.  Our permits requires us to ensure no food is taken into the gymnasiums, proper footwear is worn at all times, and gyms are left in the same condition as we find them.  Please ensure you and your teams abide by these rules.
 
  Lawrence Park - 125 Chatsworth Drive, Toronto

 
Directions:
Lawrence Park is located on the south side of Lawrence, just east of Avenue road.  It's located on Chatsworth, but you can't turn onto this street as it is a one way.

Take Greer Road south from Lawrence. Turn right on Chudleigh. Turn right onto Chatsworth Drive. The school is on your left.

Ample street parking and school parking is available.

Enter the school at the main doors off of Chatsworth.
  Eglinton Public School - Eglinton & Mt. Pleasant, Toronto

 
Directions:
Eglinton Public School is located on the south-west corner of Eglinton and Mt. Pleasant across from the Second Cup.

Free street parking is available on Roehampton (one street north of Eglinton), Brownlow (one street west of Mt. Pleasant), and Redpath (two streets west of Mt. Pleasant).

Enter the school at the Mt. Pleasant side entrance.  Doors are locked after 8:00pm.
  Sunny View Public School - 405 Blythwood Road, Toronto

 
Directions:
Sunnyview Public school is located on the north side of Blythwood east of Mt Pleasant and west of Bayview. 

Ample school parking is available at the rear of the school. 

Enter the school at the rear near the parking lot.
