Welcome to Toronto North Volleyball. We are excited about the upcoming 2002-2003 Volleyball Season. Exact dates are to be determined, but the season will start early October, 2002 and end late April, 2003. Games are held Tuesday evenings from 6:45 pm - 9:45 pm at local gyms around the Yonge and Eglinton area.  The co-ed league consists of 3 divisions: Competitive 4's, Intermediate 4's, and Recreational 6's. If you have any questions or would like to register your team early, please contract Johdi

Registration: Registration closes September 2. Registration fees will be $850 / team (Prices for individuals: $145 / Recreational, $215 / Intermediate and $215 / Competitive player). To register complete the Registration Form (right click, save target as) and email it to Steve. Cheques should be mailed to:

Toronto North Volleyball

Toronto,Ontario

Waiver and Insurance: Each player of the league must sign a Waiver Form (right click, save target as) and pay the mandatory insurance fees of $6 per player. Captain's please collect the waiver forms and fees from each player on your roster and submit the first night of the season. For information on the insurance coverage please click here. Thank you.

Captain's Meeting: There will be a Captain's Meeting at Original's Bar and Grill (Bayview) on September 10. Please be there or have a designated team member attend.

Recreation Co-ed 6's Registration Info

  • Basic Requirements: Teams of 6 with minimum of two females on the court. Must have a minimum roster of 10 people.

  • Time: Tuesdays evenings from 6:45 pm to 9:45 pm

Intermediate Co-ed 4's Registration Info

  • Basic Requirements: Teams of 4 with minimum of one female on the court. Must have a minimum roster of 6 people.

  • Time: Tuesdays evenings from 6:45 pm to 9:45 pm

Competitive Co-ed 4's Registration Info

  • Basic Requirements: Teams of 4 with minimum of one female on the court. Must have a minimum roster of 6 people.

  • Time: Tuesdays evenings from 6:45 pm to 9:45 pm
