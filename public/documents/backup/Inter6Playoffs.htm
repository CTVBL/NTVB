Intermediate 6’s - Playoffs

Playoff games will be held at Glenview and John-Ross Public Schools, Tuesday December 12.  Please have your teams at the appropriate school by 6:45 to set up the gyms and warm up.  Games must start at 7:00 pm sharp.  Teams not playing must supply referees, lines and score people.  Referees should be involved in the game.  Call the games a bit tighter, but be reasonable and fair. 

 

Finals will be held at Glenview.  The winner at John-Ross should go to Glenview when they are finished.  Target to start finals no later than 9:00pm. 

 

John-Ross

Match 1:           Singles vs. Amigos

Match 2:           Match 1 Winner vs. Markers

Final:               Winner of Match 2 must go to Glenview

 

Glenview

Match 1:           Setters vs. Tango

Match 2:           Match 1 Winner vs. Mike

Final:               John Ross Winner vs. Glenview Winner

 

Semi-Final matches will work as follows:

·         Match will consist of 1 game to 30 points.

·         Game will be traditional scoring (Not rally-point).

·         Home team serves first (e.g. Match 1, Amigos serve first).

·         Winning team must win by 2 points.

·         When you reach 15 points, break and switch sides.

 

The Final match will work as follows:

·         Glenview Winner serves first.

·         Match will be best 2 out of 3 games.

·         Games will be traditional scoring to 15 points.

·         Winning team must win by 2 points.

 

Please meet back at Original’s after the playoffs where we will award prizes to the winning teams. 

 

This is the last night of Volleyball until the New Year.  Check the Web Site, www.torontovolleyball.com, for news about changes that we will be making for the second half of the season.  Games will begin January 9, 2001.  The schedule should be posted by the end of December.
