Recreation Playoffs

 

All Recreation playoff games will be held at Glenview Public School.  Please have your teams at the school by 6:45 to set up the gym and warm up.  Games should start at 7:00 pm.  Teams not playing should supply referees, lines and score people.  Because of the strike, recreational teams may play with 3 guys and 2 girls if necessary. The current standings are:

1. Markers

2. Singles

3. Amigos

4. Tango

 

Semi-Finals – Best 2 of 3

Game 1: Markers vs. Tango

Game 2: Singles vs. Amigos

Game 3: Tango vs. Markers

Game 4: Amigos vs. Singles

Game 5: Markers vs. Tango (if necessary)

Game 6: Singles vs. Amigos (if necessary)

 

Finals – Best 3 of 5

Game 1: Winner of Markers / Tango vs. Winner of Singles / Amigos

Game 2: Winner of Singles / Amigos vs. Winner of Markers / Tango

Game 3: Winner of Markers / Tango vs. Winner of Singles / Amigos

Game 4: Winner of Singles / Amigos vs. Winner of Markers / Tango (if necessary)

Game 5: Winner of Markers / Tango vs. Winner of Singles / Amigos (if necessary)

 

·         Each game will be rally-point scoring to 25 points, win by 2, cap at 27.

·         Home team serves first (e.g. Game 1, Marker’s serve first).

·         Please meet back at Original’s after the playoffs where we will award prizes to the winning teams.
